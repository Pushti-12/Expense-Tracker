Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this app for?

This app helps you track and manage your money efficiently. It includes tools for expense tracking, financial insights, and guidance to improve your financial health.

2. How do I track my expenses?

You can navigate to the "Track Expenses" section from the dashboard and input your daily expenses. The app will categorize and summarize them for you.

3. What kind of insights can I get?

The app provides insights into your spending habits, highlights categories where you can save money, and offers personalized tips for better money management.

4. Can I interact with the chatbot for help?

Yes, you can use the chatbot to get answers to common queries, learn about app features, and get recommendations for managing your finances better.

5. Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We take data security seriously and use encryption to protect your financial information. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.
